ASUS RT-N12 VP B1 Internals

I had not planned on opening up the device but troubles with flashing OpenWRT forced my hand a bit. The case top pops off using a narrow guitar pick style pry wedge after removing two small screws from the back. Perhaps due to my lack of expertise I snapped a couple of attachment hooks but internal aesthetics aside, from the outside nothing about this tinkering is obvious.

Desoldering the UART contacts proved to be a pain as well, but I am currently without a good chisel tip for my iron so perhaps delivering the necessary heat just took longer than needed. Perhaps drilling them out is a better option but for me, in the end it worked. Pictures of the board are below before and after adding header pins and a boot log snip form the 21.02 install systemupgrade image. Enjoy?

For reference, the baud rate for the ports is 57600.
